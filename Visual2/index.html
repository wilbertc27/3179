Global Confirmed Cases from 22 Jan 2020 to 27 Jul 2020

COVID-19 Global Confirmed Cases show the total cases of each country from 22 Jan to 27 Jul 2020 per 1 Millilon Population. From the chart, we can observe that from this time period, Qatar has the highest cases among all the countries which is 39922 cases followed by Chile

. Annotated countries in the graph have more than 10000 cases per 1M population.

Death Confirmed Cases Ratio

Death confirmed cases ratio graph show the rate between the death and confirmed cases among all different countries. Countries that are being annotated are those that have more than 10000 cases per 1M population. We can observe that despite having the highest confirmed cases among all countries, Qatar has a relatively low death cases compared to other countries.

Death, Recovered and Active for different WHO Region

The graph show the death, recovered and active cases in different WHO Region. From the graph, we can observe that country that belonged to Americas has relatively high value on all category compare to other WHO Region.
